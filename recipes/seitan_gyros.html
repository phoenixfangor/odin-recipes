Seitan Gyros

 By Chris P.

TattooedMamaof2 on Food.com submitted a wonderful recipe for Seitan flavored like gyro meat and it is just delightful!

Seitan Gyro Meat recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lbs of Seitan Gryo 'meat'
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 4-6 pitas or other flatbread
  • 1 cup tzaiki sauce
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 onion, diced

Directions

  1. Heat the oil in a skillet on medium heat.
  2. Slice off thin strips of the gyro 'meat' and fry them in the oil.
  3. Once the strips are crisped to your preference, add them, some tzaiki sauce and the veggies to your flat bread.
  4. Enjoy!
